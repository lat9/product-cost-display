Based on the like-named add-on from the Zen Cart Plugins: https://www.zen-cart.com/downloads.php?do=file&id=1444

This encapsulated plugin allows you to show your product cost (the cost to you from your vendor) on the product page in the admin area. This mod only displays in the admin area and does not display on the catalog. It is great for anyone who wishes to track product cost in Zen Cart with out having to keep an external list.
